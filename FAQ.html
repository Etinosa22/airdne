Welcome to Ariadne's FAQ section. Before contacting us, please browse through the already answered questions to try and find your answers.

What is Ariadne's classroom?

Ariadne Classroom is an online educational platform that offers online classes to students and also allows teachers to upload their works. It is a project of the Team Ariadne of HNG6 internship.

How do I start?

You can start by signing up and creating an account either as a student or as a teacher or log in to an already existing account if you have one.

How do I start a course as a student?

Register or login to Ariadne. Click on courses and select a course you want to enroll for. Ariadne courses are self paced so you can work on a course at your convinient time.

How do I continue a course?

To access a course you previously enrolled for, you must be logged in to your account first. then click on your profile, scroll to the desired course and select it.

What courses will be available to me?

All courses that are enrolled for will be available. A student may also decide to view un-enrolled or new courses.

How do I post contents as a teacher?

Login or sign up using the teacher link, then navigate to the create class button on the upper right corner of your browser where details about your classes can be added.

What types of learning files are available?

Our courses are provided in pdf, videos and web document formats for now.

How old can I be to use this site?

There is no age restriction on our site, however, some courses are grouped as beginner, intermidiate or expert levels.

Does an Ariadne course provide a qualification?

At the moment, Ariadne courses do not provide a qualification and certificates are not being offered for completion of courses but we are working towards improving that.

How do I recover my password?

If you have forgotten or misplaced your password, you can click on the 'forgot password' link on the login page and follow the instructions provided.

Is Ariadne free?

Some features on Ariadne are free to use while others may require payment before they can be accessed.
